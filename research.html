Focus Areas

What We Study

Neural Engineering

Synaptic Signal Translation

Research into converting biological neural signals into machine-readable formats with <2ms latency. Applications include BCIs, prosthetic control systems, and real-time diagnostic monitoring.

Active — Since 2018 Read Paper
Neuroscience

High-Resolution 3D Brain Mapping

Building an open-access 3D atlas of the human connectome at nanometre resolution using multi-photon microscopy and AI-assisted segmentation algorithms.

Active — Since 2020 Read Paper
Biotech

Biomimetic Scaffold Design

Engineering 3D-printable tissue scaffolds that mimic extracellular matrix architecture, enabling in-vitro organ modelling for pharmaceutical drug testing pipelines.

Active — Since 2019 Read Paper
Molecular Science

Protein Folding Prediction

Developing next-generation transformer architectures to predict protein tertiary structures with >95% accuracy for novel sequences, outperforming current AlphaFold benchmarks in rare disease categories.
